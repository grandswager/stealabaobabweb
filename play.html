How to play Steal a Baobab

  1. Buy items from the red carpet OR steal from another player

  2. Your items will generate you money. Collect money from items

  3. Remember! Others can steal from you as well! Use the bat in your inventory to beat those theives up!

  4. Reach requirements to rebirth by clicking on the rebirth button

  5. Collect all the items, and become the Number 1 Steal a Baobab Player!
